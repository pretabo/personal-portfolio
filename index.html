PROFILE

I solve complex problems, streamline workflows, and drive meaningful impact for technical platform and customer-facing products with a long-term strategic thinking approach.

The Pillars Of My Career

Platform & Infrastructure

Owning CMS strategy, content modelling, and phased migration planning within business-critical publishing systems. Aligning editorial, engineering, and leadership stakeholders to evolve core infrastructure without disrupting delivery.

Front-End & Experience

Delivering user-facing improvements within content-heavy systems, collaborating closely with engineers and designers to translate product intent into robust, usable interfaces.

High-Impact Delivery

Shipping in high-traffic, nationally visible contexts — including coverage of the 2020 US election, short-form video, core news site development, and recommendation tooling — where clarity of scope, coordination, and risk management are essential.

How I Work

I'm comfortable operating in ambiguity, aligning diverse stakeholders, and working closely with engineers to translate strategy into delivery. I care about clarity, ownership, and long-term thinking — not just shipping features, but building systems that last.

  • Own the outcome — accountable from problem framing through to release.
  • Make trade-offs visible — clarity over consensus, decisions over drift.
  • Think in systems — features serve platforms, not the other way around.
  • Build for longevity — short-term wins shouldn't create long-term fragility.
