BBC Monitoring

Business Analyst

I helped rebuild the BBC Monitoring subscriber experience and the publishing tools behind it. The focus was speed, resilience, and clarity for teams working under real operational constraints.

Scope

Subscriber portal rebuild, search overhaul, bespoke CMS, international bureau rollout.

Constraints

Low connectivity, sensitive data.

Outcome

A platform teams could depend on for speed, resilience, and clarity.

Stakeholders

Product, engineering, BBC and customer teams across Monitoring and News.

Capabilities

Portal rebuild

Search & discovery

CMS platform

Led the redesign and release of the portal in React, including articles, a reworked homepage, and improved discovery.

Overhauled search so customers could find what they needed faster, with clearer filtering and more reliable results.

Created a bespoke CMS and curation tooling, built for resilience and iterative change as needs evolved.

A lot of the job was turning messy real-world constraints into something teams could rely on.
